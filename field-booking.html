📋Full Day Special Rate

Effective Rate: $25/hr

If you commit to a full day booking, you lock in at just $25/hr.

If you cancel and drop to a single slot, pricing reverts back to the standard $40/hr (morning) or $20/hr (afternoon) rates.

❌Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations must be made at least 1 week before your game day
  • Policy applies to full day bookings only
  • You may cancel the full day or choose to keep it
  • No partial cancellations (e.g., dropping one slot from a full day)
